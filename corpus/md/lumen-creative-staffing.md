# Master Services Agreement — Creative and Contract Staffing

**Contract ID:** LCS-MSA-2025-0115
**Agency:** Lumen Creative Staffing, Inc. ("Agency")
**Client:** Contoso Marketing, LLC ("Client")
**Effective Date:** January 15, 2025

---

## 1. Services

1.1 Agency provides contract and contract-to-hire creative talent (designers, copywriters, producers, motion artists) on a per-engagement basis under individual **Work Orders** that reference this MSA.

1.2 Each Work Order specifies role, rate, duration, and deliverables. This MSA governs all Work Orders.

## 2. Term and Renewal

2.1 **Initial Term.** Two (2) years from the Effective Date, ending **January 14, 2027**.

2.2 **No Automatic Renewal.** This MSA does **not** renew automatically; the parties may extend it by a signed amendment. Renewal/expiry reference date: **January 15, 2027**. Work Orders active at expiry continue under this MSA's terms until completed.

2.3 **Notice.** Either party may terminate this MSA, or any Work Order, on **thirty (30) days'** written notice.

## 3. Rates and Escalation

3.1 Talent is billed at the hourly rates in the **Rate Card (Schedule 1)**, currently ranging from **$120/hr to $180/hr** by role. There is no fixed annual platform fee; total spend is variable and engagement-driven (historical annual spend approximately **$210,000**).

3.2 **Rate Escalation.** The Rate Card increases by up to **four percent (4%)** annually on each anniversary of the Effective Date, communicated thirty (30) days in advance.

3.3 Invoices are issued bi-weekly per approved timesheets, **net 30**.

## 4. Worker Classification and Confidentiality

4.1 Agency personnel are Agency's employees or contractors, not Client's. Agency is responsible for their wages, taxes, and statutory benefits.

4.2 Each placed worker signs Client's confidentiality and IP-assignment terms before starting. Work product is assigned to Client upon creation.

## 5. Data

5.1 Agency processes limited personal data of its own personnel and Client contacts for staffing administration only. **No full Data Processing Addendum applies**, but Agency agrees to keep such data confidential and secure and to notify Client of any breach affecting Client personnel data.

## 6. Liability

Agency's liability is limited to fees paid for the affected Work Order in the prior six months, except for breaches of confidentiality or IP assignment.

## 7. Governing Law

Governed by the laws of the State of Washington.
