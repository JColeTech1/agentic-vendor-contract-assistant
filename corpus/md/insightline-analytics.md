# Marketing Analytics Platform Services Agreement

**Contract ID:** ISL-ANL-2025-0820
**Provider:** Insightline Analytics Corp. ("Insightline")
**Client:** Contoso Marketing, LLC ("Client")
**Effective Date:** August 20, 2025

---

## 1. Services

1.1 Insightline provides a hosted marketing-analytics platform for campaign attribution, audience segmentation, and cross-channel reporting ("Platform"), plus quarterly analyst review sessions.

1.2 The Platform ingests Client's first-party marketing and campaign data and produces dashboards and exports.

## 2. Term and Renewal

2.1 **Fixed Term.** This Agreement runs for twelve (12) months, from the Effective Date through **August 19, 2026**.

2.2 **No Automatic Renewal.** This Agreement does **not** renew automatically. It will expire at the end of the term **unless the parties sign a written renewal order**. The renewal/expiry date is **August 20, 2026**.

2.3 **Renewal Notice.** A party wishing to renew should give written notice of intent **at least sixty (60) days** before expiry (by **June 21, 2026**) so a renewal order can be negotiated; otherwise access ends at term and data is exported per Section 4.3.

## 3. Fees

3.1 The annual platform fee is **USD $54,000**, invoiced in two semi-annual installments, **net 30**.

3.2 **No automatic price escalation** applies. Any change to fees for a renewal term must be mutually agreed in the renewal order.

## 4. Data Protection

4.1 Insightline processes Client personal data (including marketing audience identifiers and behavioral data) as a **processor** under the **Data Processing Addendum** at **Schedule 2**, which forms part of this Agreement.

4.2 Insightline shall implement appropriate security measures, restrict sub-processors to those listed in Schedule 2-A, and notify Client of a personal-data breach without undue delay and in any event within seventy-two (72) hours.

4.3 On expiry or termination, Insightline will provide a full data export in a machine-readable format and delete Client data within thirty (30) days of confirmation.

## 5. Confidentiality

Standard mutual confidentiality applies for the term and three years thereafter.

## 6. Liability

Aggregate liability capped at the fees paid in the prior twelve months, except for breach of Section 4, which is capped at two times such fees.

## 7. Governing Law

Governed by the laws of the Commonwealth of Massachusetts.
