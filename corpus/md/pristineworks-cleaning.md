# Janitorial & Facility Cleaning Services Agreement

**Contract Ref.:** PW-SVC-2025-0312
**Service Provider:** PristineWorks Facility Services LLC ("Contractor"), 77 Industrial Way, Tukwila, WA 98188
**Client:** Contoso Marketing, LLC ("Client"), 1450 Harborview Avenue, Suite 600, Seattle, WA 98101

**Effective Date:** September 1, 2025

---

## 1. Scope of Services

1.1 Contractor shall provide janitorial and general cleaning services for Client's leased office premises (approximately 22,000 sq. ft.), as detailed in **Schedule 1 (Cleaning Specification)**, including:

- Nightly cleaning, Monday through Friday (vacuuming, trash removal, restroom sanitation, kitchen/break-room cleaning, common-area wipe-down).
- Weekly detail cleaning (glass, interior partitions, high-touch surfaces).
- Monthly floor care (buffing and carpet spot-treatment).

1.2 Supplies and consumables (paper products, soap, liners) are included unless otherwise noted in Schedule 1.

## 2. Term and Renewal

2.1 **Term.** This Agreement begins on the Effective Date and continues for a fixed term of **one (1) year**, expiring **August 31, 2026**.

2.2 **No Automatic Renewal.** This Agreement does **not** auto-renew. Renewal for any further term requires a **mutually executed written renewal or new agreement**. If no renewal is signed, services end at expiration.

2.3 **Renewal Discussion Notice.** Either party intending to renew should notify the other of that intent **at least sixty (60) days** before expiration (i.e., on or before **July 2, 2026**) to allow time to negotiate and execute renewal terms. This notice is a courtesy planning step, not an automatic extension.

## 3. Fees and Payment

3.1 **Service Fee.** Client shall pay a flat **USD $2,400 per month** (**USD $28,800 annually**), invoiced monthly in arrears.

3.2 Payment is due **net thirty (30) days**.

3.3 One-time or special-project cleaning (post-event, post-construction) is quoted and billed separately.

## 4. Pricing

4.1 The monthly fee is **fixed for the full term**. There is **no automatic price escalation** during the term.

4.2 Any change to pricing for a renewal term shall be negotiated and set out in the written renewal under Section 2.2.

## 5. Personnel and Access

5.1 Contractor's staff shall be properly screened, trained, insured, and supervised. Contractor remains the employer of record for all cleaning personnel.

5.2 Client shall provide reasonable after-hours building access. Contractor shall safeguard keys, access cards, and alarm codes and return all access items upon termination.

## 6. Insurance

Contractor shall maintain commercial general liability insurance of not less than **$1,000,000 per occurrence** and workers' compensation as required by law, and shall name Client as an additional insured.

## 7. Confidentiality

Contractor and its personnel shall not disclose any business information observed on the premises. **This Agreement does not involve Contractor processing Client's personal or customer data, and no Data Processing Addendum applies.**

## 8. Termination

8.1 Either party may terminate for convenience upon **thirty (30) days'** written notice.

8.2 Either party may terminate immediately for material breach uncured within **fifteen (15) days** of written notice.

## 9. Liability

Contractor's aggregate liability is limited to **two (2) months of service fees**, except for claims arising from gross negligence, willful misconduct, or bodily injury/property damage covered by Section 6 insurance.

## 10. Governing Law

This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Washington.
