# Managed IT Services Agreement

**Contract ID:** SHV-MSP-2024-0915
**Provider:** SecureHaven IT Partners, LLC ("Provider")
**Client:** Contoso Marketing, LLC ("Client")
**Effective Date:** September 15, 2024

---

## 1. Services

1.1 Provider delivers managed IT services for Client's ~150 endpoints and supporting infrastructure: help desk, endpoint management, patching, backup, network monitoring, and managed endpoint security (EDR), per the **Service Description (Schedule 1)**.

1.2 Provider maintains a Security Operations function providing 24×7 monitoring and incident response.

## 2. Term, Renewal, and Notice

2.1 **Initial Term.** Two (2) years from the Effective Date, ending **September 14, 2026**.

2.2 **Automatic Renewal.** The Agreement **renews automatically** for successive one-year Renewal Terms unless either party gives notice of non-renewal. Renewal date: **September 15, 2026**.

2.3 **Notice.** Non-renewal requires written notice **at least ninety (90) days** before the renewal date — by **June 17, 2026** for the upcoming term.

## 3. Fees and Adjustment

3.1 The managed-services fee is **USD $13,000 per month** (**USD $156,000 per year**), invoiced monthly, **net 30**. Project work outside the managed scope is quoted separately.

3.2 **CPI Adjustment.** At each renewal, the monthly fee is adjusted by the trailing-twelve-month change in **CPI-U**, plus up to **three percent (3%)** to reflect tooling and labor costs.

## 4. Data Protection and Security

4.1 Provider accesses Client systems containing **personal data and confidential business information**. A **Data Processing Addendum (Exhibit B)** and a **Security Addendum (Exhibit C)** form part of this Agreement.

4.2 Provider shall: enforce least-privilege and MFA for all administrative access; encrypt backups; maintain an incident-response plan; report security incidents affecting Client data within twenty-four (24) hours; and support Client's own compliance obligations.

4.3 Provider personnel are subject to background checks and confidentiality obligations.

## 5. Service Levels

Priority-1 response within 15 minutes; resolution targets per the SLA Schedule. Credits are the exclusive remedy for missed SLAs.

## 6. Liability

Aggregate liability capped at the fees paid in the prior twelve months, except for breaches of Sections 4 (data/security) and confidentiality, which are capped at two times such fees.

## 7. Governing Law

Governed by the laws of the State of Washington.
