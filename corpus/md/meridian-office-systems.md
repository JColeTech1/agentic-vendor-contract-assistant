# Office Equipment Lease Agreement (Copiers / Multifunction Printers)

**Contract ID:** MOS-LSE-2022-0101
**Lessor:** Meridian Office Systems, LLC ("Lessor")
**Lessee:** Contoso Marketing, LLC ("Lessee")
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2022

---

## 1. Equipment

1.1 Lessor leases to Lessee six (6) multifunction printer/copier units (the "Equipment") listed in **Schedule A**, including installation and networked configuration.

1.2 The lease includes a full-service maintenance plan: parts, labor, and toner, with a guaranteed four-business-hour response for service calls.

## 2. Lease Term and End of Term

2.1 **Lease Term.** Sixty (60) months (five years) from the Effective Date, ending **December 31, 2026**.

2.2 **No Automatic Renewal.** This is a fixed-term equipment lease. It does **not** auto-renew. At the end of the term, Lessee shall, at its election: (a) return the Equipment; (b) purchase the Equipment at the then-current fair market value; or (c) enter a new lease.

2.3 **End-of-Term Notice.** Lessee must give written notice of its end-of-term election **at least ninety (90) days** before the term-end date (by **October 2, 2026**). If no notice is given, the Equipment continues on a **month-to-month holdover** at the same monthly rate until returned.

## 3. Payments

3.1 The monthly lease payment is **USD $1,500** (**USD $18,000 per year**), invoiced monthly, **net 30**, for the full term.

3.2 **Fixed Pricing.** The monthly payment is **fixed for the entire lease term — there is no annual escalation**. Per-page overage above the included volume is billed quarterly at the rate in Schedule A.

## 4. Maintenance and Risk

4.1 Lessor maintains the Equipment in working order. Lessee bears risk of loss for damage beyond normal wear and must insure the Equipment at replacement value.

## 5. Data Security

5.1 The Equipment contains internal storage. **No Data Processing Addendum applies**; however, on return or purchase, Lessor shall perform a certified data-wipe of all device storage and provide a certificate of erasure.

## 6. Governing Law

Governed by the laws of the State of Illinois.
