## 1. License

1.1 Licensor grants Licensee an enterprise subscription to the **ArchivePix** library of stock photography, video, audio, and templates, with unlimited standard-license downloads for **up to 50 active users**.

1.2 Licensed assets may be used in Licensee's and its clients' marketing materials under the **Extended Commercial License** terms in Schedule A. Assets may not be resold as stand-alone files.

## 2. Term and Renewal

2.1 **Initial Term.** Twelve (12) months from the Effective Date, ending **August 9, 2026**.

2.2 **Automatic Renewal.** The subscription **renews automatically** for additional twelve-month terms unless cancelled. Renewal date: **August 10, 2026**.

2.3 **Notice.** Cancellation requires written notice **at least thirty (30) days** before the renewal date (by **July 11, 2026**).

## 3. Fees and Increase

3.1 The annual subscription fee is **USD $9,600**, billed annually in advance, **net 30**.

3.2 **Renewal Increase.** Each renewal fee increases by **ten percent (10%)** over the prior term. The increased fee is reflected in the renewal invoice.

## 4. Rights on Termination

4.1 Assets downloaded and incorporated into published materials during an active term remain licensed in perpetuity for those materials. Access to the library and new downloads ends at termination.

## 5. Data

5.1 Licensor processes only basic account and download-activity data. **No Data Processing Addendum applies.** Licensor maintains reasonable security over account information.

## 6. Liability

Licensor warrants it holds the rights to license the assets and will indemnify Licensee against third-party IP claims arising from properly licensed use. Other liability is capped at the fees paid in the prior twelve months.
