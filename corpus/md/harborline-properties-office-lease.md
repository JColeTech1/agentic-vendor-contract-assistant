# Commercial Office Lease Agreement

**Contract ID:** HLP-LSE-2022-0701
**Landlord:** Harborline Properties, LP ("Landlord")
**Tenant:** Contoso Marketing, LLC ("Tenant")
**Premises:** 1450 Harborview Avenue, Suite 600, Seattle, WA 98101 (approx. 22,000 rentable sq ft)
**Effective Date (Commencement):** July 1, 2022

---

## 1. Premises and Use

1.1 Landlord leases to Tenant the Premises for use as general and creative office space, together with the right to use common areas and **30 parking spaces**.

## 2. Term, Renewal Option, and Notice

2.1 **Primary Term.** Five (5) years from the Commencement Date, ending **June 30, 2027**.

2.2 **Renewal Option (Manual).** Tenant holds **one option to renew** for an additional five-year term at the then-prevailing terms set out in Section 3. This lease does **not** auto-renew; renewal occurs only if Tenant exercises the option. Option/renewal reference date: **July 1, 2027**.

2.3 **Notice to Exercise.** Tenant must exercise the renewal option by written notice **no fewer than one hundred twenty (120) days** before the end of the Primary Term — by **March 2, 2027**. If Tenant does not exercise the option, the lease expires and Tenant shall surrender the Premises.

2.4 **Holdover.** Holding over without renewal is at 150% of the then-current monthly base rent.

## 3. Rent and Escalation

3.1 **Base Rent.** Current base rent is **USD $32,500 per month** (**USD $390,000 per year**), payable monthly in advance on the first of each month.

3.2 **Annual Escalation.** Base rent increases by a fixed **three percent (3%)** on each anniversary of the Commencement Date for the duration of the term and any renewal term.

3.3 **Operating Expenses.** Tenant pays its pro-rata share of building operating expenses and real-estate taxes (CAM) as additional rent, reconciled annually.

## 4. Insurance and Indemnity

4.1 Tenant shall maintain commercial general liability insurance of at least $2,000,000 per occurrence and property insurance on its improvements and contents, naming Landlord as additional insured.

4.2 Tenant indemnifies Landlord against claims arising from Tenant's use of the Premises, except to the extent caused by Landlord's negligence.

## 5. Maintenance and Alterations

Landlord maintains the building structure and systems; Tenant maintains the interior of the Premises. Alterations require Landlord's prior written consent.

## 6. Data Protection

6.1 This is a real-property lease. Landlord does not process Tenant personal data in connection with the tenancy, and **no Data Processing Addendum applies**.

## 7. Governing Law

Governed by the laws of the State of Washington.
