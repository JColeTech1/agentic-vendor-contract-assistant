# Work Management Platform Subscription Agreement

**Order No.:** CAD-WOS-2024-0901
**Provider:** Cadence Work OS, Inc. ("Cadence"), with principal offices at 410 Riverside Plaza, Suite 1200, Chicago, IL 60606
**Customer:** Contoso Marketing, LLC ("Customer"), 1450 Harborview Avenue, Suite 600, Seattle, WA 98101

**Effective Date:** September 1, 2024

---

## 1. Subscription Grant

1.1 Cadence grants Customer a non-exclusive, non-transferable right to access the **Cadence Work OS** platform (the "Service") — a project and work-management tool providing task boards, Gantt timelines, resource planning, workload views, and reporting.

1.2 **Licensed Seats.** The subscription covers **80 named-user seats**, provisioned primarily for Customer's media-buying and performance-marketing team. Additional seats may be added mid-term at the then-current per-seat rate, co-terminating with the current term.

## 2. Term and Renewal

2.1 **Initial Term.** Two (2) years from the Effective Date, expiring **August 31, 2026**.

2.2 **Automatic Renewal.** The subscription **renews automatically** for successive one (1) year terms unless either party delivers written notice of non-renewal **at least sixty (60) days** before the end of the then-current term. The renewal date is **September 1, 2026**, and the non-renewal notice deadline for the upcoming term is **July 2, 2026**.

2.3 Either party may terminate for material breach uncured within thirty (30) days of written notice.

## 3. Fees

3.1 **Subscription Fee.** Customer shall pay **USD $360 per seat per year**, totaling **USD $28,800 annually** for 80 seats, invoiced annually in advance, **net 30**.

3.2 **Renewal Increase.** Upon each renewal, the per-seat fee increases by a fixed **six percent (6%)** over the prior term's per-seat rate.

## 4. Data Protection

4.1 Cadence processes Customer personal data contained in projects, tasks, and user accounts as a **processor** under the **Data Processing Addendum (Exhibit A)**, which is incorporated into and forms part of this Agreement.

4.2 Cadence shall: process Customer Data only on Customer's documented instructions; maintain SOC 2 Type II security controls with encryption in transit and at rest; restrict sub-processors to those listed in the DPA; and notify Customer of any confirmed personal-data breach without undue delay and in any event within seventy-two (72) hours.

4.3 On termination, Customer may export its data for thirty (30) days, after which Cadence deletes it.

## 5. Support

Standard support (email + in-app, business hours) is included. A 24/7 premium tier is available under a separate order.

## 6. Warranties and Liability

The Service is provided on a commercially reasonable-efforts basis. Cadence's aggregate liability shall not exceed the fees paid in the prior twelve (12) months, except for breaches of Section 4. Neither party is liable for indirect or consequential damages.

This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Illinois.
