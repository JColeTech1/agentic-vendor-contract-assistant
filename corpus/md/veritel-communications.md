# Telecommunications and Connectivity Services Agreement

**Contract ID:** VTL-TEL-2024-1015
**Provider:** Veritel Communications, Inc. ("Veritel")
**Customer:** Contoso Marketing, LLC ("Customer")
**Effective Date:** October 15, 2024

---

## 1. Services

1.1 Veritel provides business internet (1 Gbps dedicated fiber), a hosted VoIP phone system for 160 extensions, and SD-WAN management for Customer's office.

1.2 Service installation and number porting were completed at activation.

## 2. Term, Renewal, and Notice

2.1 **Initial Term.** Twenty-four (24) months from the Effective Date, ending **October 14, 2026**.

2.2 **Automatic Renewal.** Upon expiry, service **renews automatically** for successive twelve-month terms unless cancelled. Renewal date: **October 15, 2026**.

2.3 **Cancellation Notice.** Customer must provide written notice of non-renewal **at least sixty (60) days** before the renewal date (by **August 16, 2026**). Early termination during a term incurs the remaining-term charges.

## 3. Charges

3.1 Recurring monthly charges total **USD $3,000** (**USD $36,000 per year**), invoiced monthly, **net 30**. Usage-based long-distance and overage charges are billed as incurred.

3.2 **Renewal Price Change.** Veritel may adjust recurring charges for any Renewal Term. Veritel will state the new monthly charge in the renewal notice; any increase will not exceed **eight percent (8%)** over the prior term's recurring charge.

## 4. Data Handling

4.1 Veritel processes call-detail records and limited account information necessary to deliver and bill the services. Veritel does not act as a processor of Customer's marketing or end-customer personal data, and **no Data Processing Addendum is in effect**. Veritel maintains carrier-standard confidentiality of communications as required by law.

## 5. Service Levels

99.9% network availability for the dedicated circuit; credits per the SLA are the sole remedy.

## 6. Liability

Liability for service interruptions is limited to applicable service credits. Aggregate liability otherwise capped at the charges paid in the prior twelve months.

## 7. Governing Law

Governed by the laws of the State of Georgia.
