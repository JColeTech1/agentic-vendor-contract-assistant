# CRM and Marketing Automation Master Subscription Agreement

**Contract ID:** RLY-CRM-2024-0201
**Provider:** Relayworks CRM, Inc. ("Relayworks")
**Customer:** Contoso Marketing, LLC ("Customer")
**Effective Date:** February 1, 2024

---

## 1. Scope

1.1 Relayworks provides a cloud CRM and marketing-automation platform ("Service") covering contact management, lead scoring, email/SMS automation, and pipeline reporting for **200 users** and up to **750,000 contact records**.

1.2 Implementation and data-migration services were delivered under a separate, completed Statement of Work.

## 2. Term, Renewal, and Notice

2.1 **Initial Term.** Three (3) years from the Effective Date, expiring **January 31, 2027**.

2.2 **Automatic Renewal.** The Service **renews automatically** for one-year Renewal Terms. Next renewal date: **February 1, 2027**.

2.3 **Notice.** Either party may elect not to renew by written notice **at least ninety (90) days** before the renewal date (by **November 3, 2026** for the upcoming term).

## 3. Fees and Adjustment

3.1 The annual subscription fee is **USD $96,000**, invoiced annually in advance, **net 30**.

3.2 **CPI Adjustment.** At each renewal, the fee adjusts by the percentage change in the **Consumer Price Index (CPI-U)** over the trailing twelve months. If CPI change is negative, the fee remains unchanged (no decrease).

3.3 Contact-record overages above 750,000 are billed per the overage schedule.

## 4. Data Protection

4.1 Customer Data includes extensive end-customer **personal data**. Relayworks acts as **processor** under the **Data Processing Addendum (Exhibit C)**, which includes Standard Contractual Clauses where applicable.

4.2 Relayworks shall: process only on Customer's instructions; maintain ISO 27001-aligned security; encrypt personal data in transit and at rest; conduct annual penetration testing; and report personal-data breaches within seventy-two (72) hours.

4.3 Relayworks shall assist Customer with data-subject requests and, on termination, return or delete personal data within sixty (60) days at Customer's election.

## 5. Service Levels

99.9% monthly uptime; service credits per the SLA Schedule are the exclusive remedy.

## 6. Limitation of Liability

Liability capped at the fees paid in the prior twelve months, except claims arising from Section 4 (data protection) and confidentiality, which are capped at two times such fees.

## 7. Governing Law

Governed by the laws of the State of California.
