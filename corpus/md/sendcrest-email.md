# Email Service Provider (ESP) Platform Agreement

**Contract ID:** SNC-ESP-2024-0731
**Provider:** Sendcrest Email, Inc. ("Sendcrest")
**Customer:** Contoso Marketing, Inc. ("Customer")
**Effective Date:** July 31, 2024

---

## 1. Services

1.1 Sendcrest provides a hosted email marketing and transactional-sending platform, including list management, automation journeys, deliverability tooling, and analytics, for sending on behalf of Customer and its clients.

1.2 The plan includes a committed volume of **6,000,000 emails per month** across all sending domains.

## 2. Term, Renewal, and Notice

2.1 **Initial Term.** Two (2) years from the Effective Date, ending **July 30, 2026**.

2.2 **Automatic Renewal.** The Agreement **renews automatically** for one-year terms unless either party gives notice of non-renewal. Renewal date: **July 31, 2026**.

2.3 **Notice.** Written notice of non-renewal is required **at least sixty (60) days** before the renewal date — by **June 1, 2026** for the current cycle.

## 3. Fees and Usage-Based Pricing

3.1 The base platform fee is **USD $30,000 per year**, invoiced annually in advance, **net 30**.

3.2 **Usage Escalation.** Sending above the committed monthly volume is billed at **$0.50 per additional 1,000 emails**. The committed-volume tier and base fee are re-evaluated at each renewal based on the trailing twelve months' average usage; sustained higher usage moves Customer to the next pricing tier at renewal.

## 4. Data Protection

4.1 Sendcrest processes **subscriber personal data** (email addresses, engagement events, and any personal data in campaign content) as a **processor** under the **Data Processing Addendum (Exhibit A)**, which includes sub-processor terms and Standard Contractual Clauses where applicable.

4.2 Sendcrest shall: process only on Customer's instructions; honor unsubscribe and suppression obligations; maintain SOC 2 Type II security; and notify Customer of any personal-data breach within seventy-two (72) hours.

4.3 Customer remains the controller and is responsible for lawful consent to send. On termination, subscriber data is exported and deleted within thirty (30) days.

## 5. Acceptable Use

Customer shall comply with anti-spam laws (CAN-SPAM and equivalents). Sendcrest may suspend sending for verified abuse complaints.

## 6. Liability

Aggregate liability capped at the fees paid in the prior twelve months, except for breaches of Section 4.

## 7. Governing Law

Governed by the laws of the State of Colorado.
